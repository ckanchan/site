Using the Glossary

The built in glossary provides support for looking up translations for the Akkadian terms used in the accompanying texts, as well as searching for all instances of a given term. Unlike the Oracc interface, it does not yet offer the ability to perform more refined lookups, such as by written or normalised forms.

1 Lemma List Lists the lemmas in the SAAo Glossary, sorted by their dictionary citation form.
2 Search box Search lemmas by citation form or by English guideword.
3 Search in texts Searches for all occurrences of this lemma in the builtin text database, then displays the results as a virtual catalogue in the Catalogue window. These texts can be viewed in the Text Viewer, with instances of the search term highlighted.
4 Citation Form The dictionary form for a given lemma, equivalent to its form in the Concise Dictionary of Akkadian.
5 Guide Word Base translation for a citation form.
6 Written forms A list of all forms this word is written as in the texts.
7 Normalised forms A list of all forms this word has been normalised to by editors
8 Translations Shows all contextual translations for this lemma.
9 Oracc Metadata Shows the unique 'headword' and ID for the lemma, for diagnostic purposes only.
