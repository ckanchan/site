First Steps

SAAo-SX is a macOS app designed to provide a fast, offline-first interface to the State Archives of Assyria projects and other cuneiform texts. It has three main interfaces:

Catalogue

The catalogue window simply displays a list of text in its left pane, and metadata about that selected text in the right. You can select a text for reading from this window, bookmark it or open its Oracc edition in your default browser. SAAo-SX comes with a builtin offline database incorporating many of the texts included in the State Archives of Assyria project. Alternatively, SAAo-SX can also download catalogues and their accompanying texts from the internet. Read more

Text Viewer

The text viewer displays cuneiform texts. It is designed to make it easy to view a text in any of its possible representations: as a transliteration, normalisation, translation, or 'cuneified' approximation. It loads texts selected from the Catalogue window, but can also read texts opened directly. Read more

Glossary

Browse the computer-generated SAAo glossary, and look up all texts which contain a given lemma. Read more
