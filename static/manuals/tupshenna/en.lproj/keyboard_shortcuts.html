Keyboard shortcuts

On the iPad, you can use a connected keyboard to navigate through the Tupšenna interface. Press and hold the ⌘ key to see all available shortcuts, which are also listed here.

⌘← Moves to the previous text.
⌘→ Moves to the next text.
⌘I Shows the Text Info view.
⌘1 Show cuneiform in primary (left) column.
⌘2 Show transliteration in primary (left) column.
⌘3 Show normalisation in primary (left) column.
⌘4 Show translation in primary (left) column.
⌥1 Show cuneiform in secondary (right) column (if visible).
⌥2 Show transliteration in secondary (right) column (if visible).
⌥3 Show normalisation in secondary (right) column (if visible).
⌥4 Show translation in secondary (right) column (if visible).
