Oracc

The Open Richly Annotated Cuneiform Corpus ('Oracc') is an international scholarly effort to compile and make freely available accurate and interactive editions of cuneiform texts. It serves as a repository for many Akkadian and Sumerian texts, as well as glossaries and informational sites.
